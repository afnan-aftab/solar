IoT Based Home Solar Power System

The ever increasing energy crisis has necessitated the quest of alternate energy sources and their efficient management by leveraging the modern information technology tools and techniques. Although several efficient solar power solutions for home and industry have been developed in the past few years, there is still need for further improvements to render these solutions more economically viable.

We propose to employ modern machine learning/deep learning algorithms to predict the future usage and electricity production of solar panels and use these insights to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of the home solar power systems. The project would entail developing a bread board solution, recording the important solar panel parameters like current, voltage and temperature using sensors and transmitting the information to a cloud using Arduino. Machine learning/deep learning algorithms can thereafter be applied on the recorded data to gain predictive insights and gainfully employ them to enhance the performance and effectiveness of the system.
